Property Description

Experience the pinnacle of Hollywood Hills living in this newly constructed minimalist masterpiece. Designed by renowned architectural firm Studio Apex, this residence perfectly blends brutalist elements with warm Scandinavian interior design.

The open-concept main floor features soaring 12-foot ceilings and floor-to-ceiling glass walls that disappear into the structure, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor flow. The professional chef's kitchen is equipped with custom white oak cabinetry and Gaggenau appliances.

Outside, the sprawling terrace hosts an infinity-edge salt water pool, outdoor kitchen, and fire pit area—all overlooking the iconic Los Angeles skyline from downtown to the Pacific Ocean.
